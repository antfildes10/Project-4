Introduction

At KartControl, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our go-kart racing booking platform.

Information We Collect

When you register for an account, we collect personal information including your name, email address, phone number, and username. When you make bookings, we also collect information about your session preferences, booking history, and payment details. We automatically collect technical information such as IP addresses, browser types, and device information to improve our service and enhance security.

How We Use Your Information

We use your personal information to process and manage your racing session bookings, communicate with you about your bookings and account updates, and improve our services based on user behavior and preferences. We may also use your information to send you promotional materials about upcoming events and special offers, though you can opt out of these communications at any time.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. Your password is encrypted using secure hashing algorithms, and all data transmission is protected using SSL/TLS encryption. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information at any time through your account settings. You can also request a copy of all data we hold about you or object to the processing of your data. If you have any concerns about how we handle your information, please contact us using the details on our contact page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at our contact page or email us at info@kartcontrol.com.
